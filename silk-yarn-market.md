<p><p>The silk yarn market is categorized into several types based on the origin and production methods of the silk. Eri silk yarn is derived from Eri silkworms and is known for its warmth and sustainability. Muga silk yarn, originating from Assam, is distinctive for its golden hue. The "Other" category encompasses various lesser-known silk types.</p></p>
