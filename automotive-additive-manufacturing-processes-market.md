<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion</li><li>Binder Jetting</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition</li><li>Material Extrusion</li><li>Material Jetting</li><li>Sheet Lamination</li><li>Vat Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive additive manufacturing processes market encompasses various technologies, including Powder Bed Fusion, which uses powdered materials to create parts layer by layer. Binder Jetting involves selectively applying a binding agent to powder layers. Directed Energy Deposition melts materials to build parts, while Material Extrusion deposits melted thermoplastic filament. Material Jetting sprays droplets of material to form layers. Sheet Lamination bonds sheets of material together, and Vat Polymerization uses UV light to cure liquid resins, enabling diverse, efficient production methods in the automotive industry.</p></p>
