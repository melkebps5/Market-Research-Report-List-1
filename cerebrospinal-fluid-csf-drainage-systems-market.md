<p><p>Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Drainage Systems are key in managing conditions like hydrocephalus and intracranial hypertension. The market features two primary types: Ventricular Drainage Systems and Lumbar Drainage Systems. Ventricular Drainage Systems are designed to divert excess CSF directly from the ventricles of the brain, providing rapid pressure relief. In contrast, Lumbar Drainage Systems drain fluid from the lumbar region of the spine. Both systems are essential for maintaining optimal intracranial pressure and ensuring patient safety in neurological care.</p></p>
