<p><p>The rheumatoid arthritis treatment market comprises various approaches. Symptomatic treatment focuses on relieving pain and improving joint function through analgesics and anti-inflammatory medications. Intermediate corticosteroid therapies utilize corticosteroids to reduce inflammation and suppress the immune response, offering short-term relief. Disease Modifying Antirheumatic Drug (DMARD) therapies aim to slow disease progression and prevent joint damage by targeting the underlying immune mechanisms. These distinct types collectively enhance patient quality of life and management of rheumatoid arthritis symptoms and complications.</p></p>
