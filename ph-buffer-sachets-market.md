<p><p>The pH buffer sachets market is segmented into two main types: acidic buffers and alkaline buffers. Acidic buffers maintain a stable pH in lower ranges, typically between 4 and 6, making them ideal for applications in biological and chemical processes requiring these conditions. Alkaline buffers, on the other hand, stabilize higher pH levels, often between 7 and 10, and are commonly used in various laboratory and industrial settings. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining system stability and enhancing product efficacy.</p></p>
